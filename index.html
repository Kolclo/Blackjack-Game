Blackjack


RULES:

This is a simplified version of Blackjack.

The aim of the game is to get exactly 21 points. If you exceed 21 points, you lose.

You can compare your current cards with the dealer, whoever is closest to 21 wins.

Each game costs £10 to play, but you get £30 back if you win!

Jack, Queen and King are equal to 10, Aces are equal to 11.
